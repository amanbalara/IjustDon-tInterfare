Why this is for them

This page is gentle and non-judgemental — designed to open conversation with someone who might be spending too much time or money on online betting. Share it with them or show it in person.

Signs you might notice

  • Spending more money than intended or hiding transactions
  • Reduced sleep, mood swings, secrecy, or constant checking of apps
  • Arguments about money or distancing from friends/family

How to start the talk (gentle templates)

Click a message to copy — use your own words to sound natural.

Hey, I care about you — I’ve noticed you seem stressed about betting lately. Can we talk?
I’m worried about you. I’m here if you want help setting limits or taking a break together.
You’re important to me. If you ever want to cut back, I can help block apps or sit with you when you’re tempted.

Practical steps (what you can offer)

  • Offer to block or uninstall betting apps together for a test period.
  • Set shared limits: daily/weekly money or time caps.
  • Replace the habit: sports, gym, study session, or a quick walk.
  • Help track spending for a week — non-judgmental and private.

When to encourage professional help

If they keep losing control, borrow money, or feel hopeless, suggest talking to a counsellor or a helpline. Be patient — push gently, not forcefully.
